== Installation ==

To make the plugin work you only need to do:

1. Install the plugin OR unzip the downloaded file to /wp-content/plugins/
2. Go to your plugin settings and enable the plugin.
3. In the backoffice, under settings, you will have a new section called "Sidebar Form". Go to that section and make all the modifications you need.
4. To use the plugin on your sidebar, just drag and drop the widget called "Sidebar Form" in the widget section.
5. You can also display the form to your posts or pages by inserting the shortcode [SIDEBAR-FORM] on every pages or posts you want.
6. If you are not recieving emails, you may need to turn off Allow Comments on the page where you place [SIDEBAR-FORM].
